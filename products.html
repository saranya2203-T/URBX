URBX AffordAI

Data-driven affordability intelligence for cities and housing markets

URBX AffordAI uses spatial data, policy variables, and AI models to assess housing affordability, land value stress, and access gaps. It helps planners, governments, and developers make evidence-based decisions on inclusive housing and urban investment.

URBX Flow

Understanding how cities move, grow, and interact

URBX Flow analyzes urban systems—mobility, land use, infrastructure, and population dynamics—to reveal spatial patterns and inefficiencies. It supports scenario planning, network optimization, and long-term urban strategy with clear, visual insights.

URBX Voice

Translating community voices into actionable planning insights

URBX Voice captures, analyzes, and structures public input, stakeholder feedback, and participatory data using AI-assisted tools. It helps planners integrate community perspectives into policy, design, and decision-making in a measurable way.
